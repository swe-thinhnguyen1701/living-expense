# Living Expense
This project is a living expense web app that allows users to input their income and expenses. The app will calculate and display whether the user’s finances are trending upward or downward, helping them stay informed about their financial health.

## Project Stages

**Stage One:**
The initial phase focuses on daily expense tracking. Data will be stored locally for easy access, enabling users to review and manage their daily records. The app will provide core features, including options to add, edit, and delete entries.

**Stage Two:**
In this phase, the app will integrate a database to support user accounts with login and signup features. Users will be able to generate weekly and monthly expense reports based on their inputs. For weekly and monthly reports, the app will compare the current and previous periods to show spending trends.

**Stage Three:**
The final phase will enable users to review expenses at a weekly and monthly level over a two-year period, covering the current and previous years. This feature will allow users to track spending trends over time and compare their expenses year over year.
